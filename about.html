Meet the MakerMarika Hokhold

Happiness can be as simple as a white minidress on a sunny afternoon, but don't forget the ballet slippers.
How kind of you to visit my page. I'm a Canadian-born, Hungarian-American, that loves Budapest and Paris, ballet and techno, and knows that a drawing can come to life with a few stitches.

The way we present ourselves to the world is an integral part of our identity. Colour, fabric, and texture tell the story we choose to convey.

It's this freedom of expression that drew me to fashion design from an early age, sewing my own ballet dresses, marvelling at storybook gowns, and eventually swooning over Givenchy's custom made outfits for the iconic Audrey Hepburn.
The designs I've created embody the spirit of the enchanting characters who inspired these dresses, but also have a unique twist all my own. Until now, I've primarily focused on fantasy, but my portfolio is growing.

If you've enjoyed these designs, or would like to collaborate, let me know. Feel free to reach out by submitting your email on my homepage.
Vous pouvez me trouver à la matinée. Bisous!

- Marika
